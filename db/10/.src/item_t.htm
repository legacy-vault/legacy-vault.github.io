Description Text
This Compilation Pack of Heroes of Might and Magic Games includes:
Heroes of Might and Magic;
Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Succession Wars;
Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Price of Loyalty;
Heroes of Might and Magic III: The Restoration of Erathia.

CD1: Heroes of Might and Magic for Windows v1.0 August 30, 1999 re-Release, 1 Data and 49 CD-DA Tracks. King's Bounty is included on the CD.
CD2: Heroes of Might and Magic II Gold v2.1 August 26, 1999 re-Release, 1 Data and 42 CD-DA Tracks.
CD3: Heroes of Might and Magic III: The Restoration of Erathia v1.1 August 30, 1999 re-Release (Patch 1.1 is included, but this Version has different Game Executable compared to official 1.1 Game Patch).
