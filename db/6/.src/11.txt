Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Price of Loyalty is a Scenario Add-on Pack for Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Succession Wars. It provides new Heroes, Artifacts and Events. <br>
<br>
This Release is the only stand-alone Release of the Expansion Pack, which must be installed upon the first Game of the Series (Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Succession Wars). The CD contains 1 Data-Track and 42 Audio-Tracks, but the Quality of the Soundtrack is known to be worse than it used to be in the previous Game of the Series (Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Succession Wars).
