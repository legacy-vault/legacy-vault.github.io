Heroes of Might and Magic Compendium is a Compilation of Games released by The 3DO Company in Year 1997. It contained such Games as: <br>
King's Bounty; <br>
Heroes of Might and Magic: A Strategic Quest; <br>
Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Succession Wars; <br>
Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Price of Loyalty. <br>
<br>
The first Disk contains both (DOS and Windows) Versions of the first Series of Heroes of Might and Magic. 
The second Disk contains two Parts of the second Series of Heroes of Might and Magic 
("The Succession Wars" and "The Price of Loyalty" Expansion). <br>
<br>
This Release is unique because it has Game Versions which are not available as Patches. 
In other Words, the first Disk contains DOS Version 1.3 and Windows Version 1.2 of the Heroes of Might and Magic. 
Latest official H.o.M.M. Patch for DOS Version is 1.2 and for Windows Version -- 1.1.
